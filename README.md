Buisness Card Website:
The website that I created is meant to be a personal buisness card that I would be able to use for employers and networking. The designs themselves are very simplistic and clean which allows the viewer to focus on the details that are on the website itself. These details include my name, and a subtitle showing im a graphic designer. Along with this information there are icons that allow the user to either get into contact with me or preview my work for themselves.

My Process: My process for this project began with many Figma Mock-ups that all were eirely similar while also having stark differences that made them stand out. Once I had a final design that I liked, I began grabbing assets for my website by extracting the icons and typefaces I had and made, to then put them into the HTML. Once this was done I began coding the website, and doing research on how to code.

Usability Check:
To check my Business card I sent the Link to my Roomates Luke and Lara. They both were able to open the link on their own devices. They also were both able to press all links and find where to get in contact with me if needed. 
They believed the card was for business purposes, like I would send employers this link in order for them to look at all my things along with having a way to contact me. Because of this I didn't Change much on the project because of their feedback.
